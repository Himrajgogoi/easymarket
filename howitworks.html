How It Works

An easy follow through diagram

The mechanism:

  1. Set up your account on our website and provide the necessary details.

  2. Upload your list of items available and their prices daily.

  3. The list of items will be used to search for items the user in the locality wants.

  4. Once the item/items match, your shop description will be shown to the user.

  5. If the user wants to order items online, you will be notified and then you will need to respond whether you will be able to deliver or not.
