What this site is for?

This site is an initiative to provide our local vendors a platform to provide a better and improved customer reach to counter the advantage of the big players in this market. A stage to go local, to support local, to uplift local.

Our aim

In this fast moving world, the value of time has never been greater. The ever increasing advancement in technological and scientific field has made it possible to access any part of the world through just a small hand held device. Even then sometimes we feel ourselves in derth of time. Gone are the days when you have to wait for 2-3 days or even more to lay your hands on a particular item of interest. We value your time as our own so we aim to provide fast search and delivery of products to you within your area! While also supporting the local businesses!
